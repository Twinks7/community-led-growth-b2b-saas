# Community-Led Growth for B2B SaaS

## Overview

This repository is a research project focused on Community-Led Growth (CLG) strategies for B2B SaaS companies. The goal is to understand how successful founders, marketers, and community leaders build engaged communities that drive customer acquisition, retention, and long-term business growth.

This project is part of a structured research assignment and aims to build a strong knowledge base that can later be converted into actionable growth playbooks.

---

## Research Objectives

- Understand the fundamentals of Community-Led Growth.
- Study how B2B SaaS companies build and nurture communities.
- Analyze strategies shared by experienced practitioners.
- Collect insights from LinkedIn posts, YouTube videos, podcasts, and other resources.
- Identify best practices and common mistakes in community building.

---

## Research Questions

- What is Community-Led Growth?
- Why is community important for B2B SaaS companies?
- Which platforms are most effective for building communities?
- How do successful companies engage and retain community members?
- What metrics are used to measure community success?
- What lessons can be learned from experienced community builders?
