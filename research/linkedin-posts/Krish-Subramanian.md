# Krish Subramanian

## LinkedIn Profile

https://www.linkedin.com/in/krishsubramanian/

## About

Krish Subramanian is the Co-founder and CEO of Chargebee and a founding member of SaaSBoomi. He is known for helping scale one of India's leading B2B SaaS companies while mentoring founders and contributing to the SaaS ecosystem through community-led initiatives.

---

## Research Note

While Krish shares updates on LinkedIn, many of his most valuable insights are available through Chargebee blogs, SaaSBoomi talks, podcasts, and public interviews. This research combines those publicly available resources.

---

## Public Articles and Resources

### 1. Building Global SaaS Companies from India

**Source:** SaaSBoomi

**Summary**

Krish shares lessons from building Chargebee into a global subscription management platform. He explains the importance of solving customer problems, maintaining product quality, and thinking globally from the beginning.

**Key Takeaways**

- Build products with global customers in mind.
- Customer feedback should guide product development.
- Long-term thinking creates sustainable companies.

---

### 2. Lessons in Scaling Chargebee

**Source:** Chargebee Blog

**Summary**

Krish discusses the operational challenges of scaling a SaaS company, emphasizing hiring, customer success, and product excellence. He explains that growth should never compromise customer experience.

**Key Takeaways**

- Customer success drives retention.
- Great teams build great products.
- Sustainable growth requires operational discipline.

---

### 3. Founder Mentorship through SaaSBoomi

**Source:** SaaSBoomi

**Summary**

Krish explains how founder communities accelerate learning by encouraging experienced entrepreneurs to mentor new founders. He believes collaboration strengthens the entire SaaS ecosystem.

**Key Takeaways**

- Founder communities encourage knowledge sharing.
- Mentorship reduces common startup mistakes.
- Collaboration benefits the ecosystem.

---

### 4. Product-Led Growth in SaaS

**Source:** Public Interview

**Summary**

Krish shares why product quality and customer experience should be the primary growth drivers for SaaS companies. He advocates building products customers genuinely enjoy using.

**Key Takeaways**

- Product quality drives organic growth.
- Happy customers become advocates.
- Focus on solving meaningful problems.

---

### 5. Leadership and Scaling Teams

**Source:** Podcast Interview

**Summary**

Krish discusses leadership during periods of rapid company growth, emphasizing culture, hiring, and transparent communication.

**Key Takeaways**

- Strong culture supports rapid scaling.
- Hire people who align with company values.
- Transparent leadership builds trust.
