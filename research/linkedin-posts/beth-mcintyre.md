## About

Beth McIntyre is the Director of Community at CMX. She leads community strategy, education, and events that help organizations build thriving, business-driven communities.

## Public Articles and Resources

### 1. Community Strategy: The Three Level Framework

**Source:** CMX Blog

**Link:** https://www.cmxhub.com/blog/the-three-level-strategy-framework

**Summary**

Beth introduces CMX's Three-Level Strategy Framework, explaining how successful communities should align business goals, community programs, and day-to-day execution. She emphasizes measuring community success through business outcomes rather than engagement alone.

**Key Takeaways**

- Align community initiatives with business objectives.
- Measure community impact using meaningful business metrics.
- Build strategy before executing tactics.

---

### 2. How Communities Drive Growth: Strategies for Success

**Source:** Bevy Blog

**Link:** https://bevy.com/b/blog/how-communities-drive-growth

**Summary**

Beth explains how community should be integrated into a company's go-to-market (GTM) strategy. She discusses using the SPACES framework to connect community efforts with customer acquisition, engagement, and long-term business growth.

**Key Takeaways**

- Community should support GTM strategy.
- Community programs can drive customer acquisition and loyalty.
- Business value should be measurable.

---

### 3. CMX Guide to Building a Community

**Source:** CMX Guide

**Link:** https://www.cmxhub.com/reports/the-cmx-guide-to-building-a-community

**Summary**

This guide outlines practical frameworks for planning, launching, and growing sustainable brand communities. It covers member value, business alignment, community strategy, and measurement.

**Key Takeaways**

- Start with member value and business alignment.
- Use proven frameworks like SPACES and the Three-Level Strategy Framework.
- Sustainable communities require long-term planning and measurement.
