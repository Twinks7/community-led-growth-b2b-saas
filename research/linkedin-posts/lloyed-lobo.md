# Lloyed Lobo

## LinkedIn Profile

https://www.linkedin.com/in/lloyedlobo/

## About

Lloyed Lobo is the Co-founder of Boast.ai and Traction, a community of more than 100,000 founders, operators, and investors. He is also the author of *From Grassroots to Greatness*, a book focused on building iconic brands through community-led growth.

---

## Research Note

While recent LinkedIn posts were limited during the research period, Lloyed regularly shares community-building insights through his book, podcasts, interviews, and public articles. The following resources represent his core thinking on community-led growth.

Lloyed explains that successful businesses are built by creating communities rather than simply attracting audiences. **Source:** eHandbook
