# Avinash Raghava

## LinkedIn Profile

https://www.linkedin.com/in/avinashraghava/

## About

Avinash Raghava is the CEO and Founding Volunteer of SaaSBoomi (now AIBoomi), India's largest pay-it-forward community for SaaS and AI founders. He has spent over two decades building founder communities and enabling entrepreneurs through mentorship, networking, and knowledge sharing.
