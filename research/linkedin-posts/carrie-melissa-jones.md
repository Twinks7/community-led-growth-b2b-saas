# Carrie Melissa Jones

## LinkedIn Profile

https://www.linkedin.com/in/carriemelissajones/

## About

Carrie Melissa Jones is the founder of The CMJ Group and a globally recognized community strategist. She helps organizations design intentional communities that drive engagement, belonging, and long-term business growth. She is also the co-author of *Building Brand Communities*.

## Public Articles and Resources

### 1. Building Brand Communities

**Source:** Carrie Melissa Jones Website

**Summary**

Carrie explains that successful communities are intentionally designed around shared purpose rather than simply bringing people together. She emphasizes creating meaningful member experiences and aligning community objectives with business goals.

**Key Takeaways**

- Community should be designed intentionally.
- Shared purpose creates stronger engagement.
- Business goals and community goals should align.

### 2. The Community Engagement Framework

**Source:** The CMJ Group

**Summary**

**Key Takeaways**

### 5. Measuring Community Success

**Source:** Webinar / Community Resources

**Summary**

Carrie explains that community success should be measured using both business metrics and member outcomes. She encourages organizations to evaluate engagement, retention, advocacy, and customer satisfaction rather than focusing solely on membership numbers.

- Community creates long-term business value.
