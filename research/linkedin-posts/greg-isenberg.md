# Recent LinkedIn Posts

## 1. Build startups for agents

**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/gisenberg_build-startups-for-agents-i-think-its-the-activity-7479916228963233793-rZ9V

**Summary**

Greg argues that the next major startup wave will be built for AI agents rather than humans. He highlights the need for infrastructure such as trusted memory, permission systems, payments, observability, and machine-to-machine marketplaces. His central idea is that software should increasingly be designed with AI agents as first-class users.

**Key Takeaways**

- AI agents will require dedicated infrastructure for trust, payments, and permissions.
- Documentation is becoming a critical product interface for AI.
- Machine-to-machine commerce will create entirely new business opportunities.

## 5. How to build a company that runs on AI agents

**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/gisenberg_a-friend-asked-me-how-to-actually-build-a-activity-7476685047484526592-3r9l
