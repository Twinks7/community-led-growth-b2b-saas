# Greg Isenberg

## About

Greg Isenberg is the CEO of Late Checkout and an entrepreneur focused on community-first startups, audience building, AI, and internet businesses.

---

# Video 1

## Community Building with Greg Isenberg

**Published:** June 2, 2024

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e9J9s2_DAKE

### Summary

Greg discusses how strong communities create a sustainable competitive advantage for startups. Instead of relying solely on paid marketing, he encourages founders to build spaces where customers can connect, share ideas, and contribute to the product's evolution. He explains that engaged communities improve trust, customer retention, and organic growth.

### Key Takeaways

- Community is a long-term business asset.
- Strong relationships create loyal customers.
- Communities generate valuable product feedback.
- Founder participation builds trust.
- Consistent engagement is more important than rapid growth.

# Video 2

## Community-Led Growth & Web3 – Building the Future of Internet Products

**Published:** August 26, 2022

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=deet07auY58

### Summary

Greg explains how community-led growth can become a company's primary growth engine. He discusses the importance of creating value before selling, empowering members to contribute, and building products that naturally encourage participation. He also explores how online communities influence product adoption and long-term customer loyalty.

### Key Takeaways

- Communities should create value before monetization.
- Users become advocates when they feel ownership.
- Trust drives sustainable growth.
- Community feedback improves products.
- Community-led businesses grow through relationships rather than advertising.
