# Avinash Raghava - YouTube / Podcast Research

## About
Avinash Raghava leads SaaSBoomi, a volunteer-driven community for Indian SaaS and AI founders.

## Video 1
**Title:** Everything You Need to Know About SaaSBoomi SGx!  
**Published date:** 2024-07-17  
**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GIPGIz8f6Yc  

**Summary:**  
This video is relevant because it explains SaaSBoomi SGx, a program within the SaaSBoomi ecosystem. The public description says SGx is a 16-week, implementation-focused program for SaaS startups, with no equity and no fee. For community-led growth research, this matters because it shows how SaaSBoomi converts founder community into structured founder support. The program reflects a pay-it-forward model where expertise, operating help, and peer learning are delivered through a community institution.

**5 key takeaways:**
- SaaSBoomi SGx is implementation-focused.
- The program is designed for SaaS startups.
- It operates without equity or fees, based on commitment.
- Shows community as structured founder support.
- Relevant to Indian SaaS ecosystem building.
